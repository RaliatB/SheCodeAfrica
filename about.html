About Me

Hi, I’m Raliat Bello — I didn’t always think coding was for me. Back in my university days, I was drawn to the idea of creating things with code. But as much as I loved the concept, coding felt overwhelming. The lectures made it seem so complicated. Eventually, I decided to focus on IT infrastructure engineering instead, leaving my dream of coding behind.

For years, I worked in infrastructure, gaining experience and building a career I was proud of. But deep down, I never stopped admiring the creativity and possibilities that come with coding. I knew I had unfinished business with it. That’s why joining the She Code Africa FrontEnd Development Bootcamp has been a turning point for me. It’s given me a chance to rewrite the narrative I had created for myself back in school.

Now, I’m learning to approach coding with fresh eyes, guided by a supportive community and hands-on lessons in HTML and CSS. It’s no longer about fear or perfection but about progress and creativity. This project is a personal milestone—a reminder that it’s never too late to chase the things you love. I’m excited to keep growing, building, and sharing my journey with you.
